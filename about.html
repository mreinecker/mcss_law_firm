About the Firm

Kevin is a Central Jersey native and a graduate of Rutgers School of Law. After serving three years as a Trial Attorney in the United States Department of Justice in Washington D.C., he has spent the last 30 years in private practice as a Director in one of New Jersey’s premier law firms, Lowenstein Sandler, LLC and in a local mid-sized Somerset County litigation firm, Purcell Ries. In 2006 Kevin opened his own practice in Somerville serving individuals and small businesses in Somerset, Middlesex, Hunterdon and Morris Counties. Presently he shares his office with his assistant Corry and a 3-year-old rescue dog named Newton. Newton loves when a client brings him a treat.

Civil Litigation

Kevin’s practice is limited to civil litigation:

  1. Individuals injured in automobile accidents, in slip and fall cases; wrongful death cases and product defect claims;
  2. Workers compensation claims for job related injuries;
  3. Small to mid-size businesses involved in commercial litigation, landlord tenant disputes, breach of contract claims and employment issues;
  4. Wrongful termination, discrimination, harassment and retaliation claims for officers, managers and other employees;
  5. Negotiated Separation Agreements;
  6. Non-compete and Confidential/Proprietary Information

Kevin will help you identify the issues and prepare a strategy to help you obtain the result you desire. Because of Kevin’s reputation and experience as a Certified Civil Trial Attorney, a distinction held by only approximately 2% of New Jersey Lawyers, and his status as a “Super Lawyer” appearing in New Jersey Monthly Magazine for over ten years , he is able to achieve excellent results for his clients.
